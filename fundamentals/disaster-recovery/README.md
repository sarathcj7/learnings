## Why This Matters

- **Risk mitigation**: Every company has had a disaster; preparing reduces impact
- **Regulatory**: GDPR, PCI-DSS, HIPAA require documented recovery plans
- **RTO/RPO**: Defines business value (15min downtime vs 1 hour = 10x cost difference)
- **Testing**: Untested backups are useless (80% of backups fail in real incidents)
- **Production readiness**: Chaos engineering catches bugs before customers do
